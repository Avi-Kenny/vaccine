# vaccine: Statistical Tools for Immune Correlates Analysis of Vaccine Clinical Trial Data

Various semiparametric and nonparametric statistical tools for immune
correlates analysis of vaccine clinical trial data. This includes
calculation of summary statistics and estimation of risk, vaccine
efficacy, controlled effects (controlled risk and controlled vaccine
efficacy), and mediation effects (natural direct effect, natural
indirect effect, proportion mediated). See Gilbert P, Fong Y, Kenny A,
and Carone, M (2022)
[doi:10.1093/biostatistics/kxac024](https://doi.org/10.1093/biostatistics/kxac024)
and Fay MP and Follmann DA (2023)
[doi:10.48550/arXiv.2208.06465](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2208.06465)
.
